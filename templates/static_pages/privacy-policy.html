Our Commitment to Your Privacy

At Keep it SWEET!, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we use cookies, including third-party cookies from Stripe.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device to improve your browsing experience. They help websites function properly and provide features like secure online payments.

Stripe Cookies

We use Stripe to process secure payments on our website. Stripe may place cookies on your device to ensure payment functionality, prevent fraud, and enable a seamless checkout experience.

For more information about how Stripe uses cookies, visit the Stripe Cookie Policy.

Other Cookies

In addition to Stripe cookies, we may use cookies to:

  • Analyze site traffic and improve website performance.
  • Personalize your shopping experience.
  • Provide access to special offers and promotions.

Your Choices

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. However, please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website, including the ability to make secure payments via Stripe.

Contact Us
